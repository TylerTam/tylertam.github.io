Contributions

  • VR systems developer
  • Multiplayer Programmer
  • Asset Implementation
  • Optimization

I was given the role of implementing the core VR systems while utilizing their in-studio SDK. I designed & developed the in-game menu system, the object interactivity, and the tutorial systems.

Aside from the VR aspects, I was also given the role of implementing Photon Pun 2 multiplayer, and integrating the VR systems into this as well while ensuring they were working on mobile.


Workplace Duties

While working at SpaceCard, I participated in daily standup meetings, with daily delieverables and feedback. As we were working remotely, it was difficult at first to create functional builds everyday in order to send for feedback. However, from this challenge, I was able to swiftly adjust systems and functionality, and also able to debug issues much quicker.
