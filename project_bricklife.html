Role: Technical Game Designer


Lego Fortnite - Bricklife is game being developed by Epic Games, released in December 2024 with continued content updates for the foreseeable future. It is an official collaboration project with the LEGO group, and part of the Epic developed Lego Fortnite ecosystem.




Responsibilities

  • Game Design
  • Blueprint Implementation
  • Design Point of Contact
  • Design Support

I was brought onto this project as a technical game designer, where I owned multiple game systems, mechanics, and POI designs from the prototype phase to post launch content updates. I worked alongisde several other game designers, as well as multiple other disciplines (art, ui/ux, VFX/SFX) to design and implement gameplay features that I had ownership over.

As a technical designer, I was also the main point of contact on several gameplay features and systems, acting as a bridge between design and engineering for more technically involved features and mechanics. This responsibility would involve scoping out the asks & time requirements of new features, helping other designers understand what system we had, what was possible with our existing tools, and roping in either engineering, or other disciplines, into conversations when their expertise were required. Additionally, I also assist in improving our pipelines, creating in-editor tools, work flows, or documentation + videos on existing tools / systems.

As I had experience working with many of our game systems, I was also acting as design support on features that would require additional support in order to ship, and would also help takeover work for teammates who would be absent for an extended amount of time.



Owned Features

  • Bricklife Smart Objects Designs
    • Main point of contact on design side
    • Continued to establish improvements and better workflows on smartobjects
  • Multiple POI overall designs
    • Designed & implemented interactable objects, new mechanics, AI behaviours, and progression flows for each POI
    • Collab'd with LD to create environments that matched the aesthetic, while meeting gameplay desires and perf concerns
    • Owned POIs:
      • Cozy Paws Cafe
      • Mourndale Academy
      • DJ Yonder's Night Club
  • New gameplay mechanics / Minigames
    • Designed, prototyped, and implemented new game systems / mechanics with modularilty and reusability in mind
    • Collab'd with all disciplines to scope out new features and mechanics while ensuring the original design goals were being met
    • Playtested, and balanced new minigames per user feedback
    • Created mechanics / minigames:
      • Vampire Experiment (Infection)
      • NPC + Player Requests system
      • Bounty Board quest system
